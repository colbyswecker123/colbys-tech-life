Privacy

Simple privacy information.

This site is intentionally simple and privacy-friendly.

Privacy overview

Colby's Tech Life does not use advertising trackers or customer accounts on this website. The site is primarily informational and designed to let visitors request help.

Contact form information

If you submit the contact form, the information you provide may be used to respond to your request. This can include your name, email, phone number, service category, timeline, and message details.

Email and phone contact

If you contact Colby's Tech Life directly by email, phone, or text, the information you share will be used to respond to your request.

Hosting

The website is designed for Cloudflare Pages. Basic technical information may be processed by Cloudflare as part of normal website hosting and security.

Questions

For privacy questions, email colbystechlife@gmail.com.
