FAQ

Common questions about working with Colby’s Tech Life.

Simple answers for websites, support, domains, automation, and quote requests.

What kind of tech help do you offer?

I help with small business websites, website updates, domains, Cloudflare setup, contact forms, basic SEO setup, spreadsheet cleanup, data/reporting help, workflow automation, troubleshooting, and general digital support.

Do you work with small businesses?

Yes. The site is built especially for individuals, families, side businesses, churches, nonprofits, local organizations, and small businesses that need practical help without an overly complicated process.

Can you build a website for me?

Yes. I can help with clean informational websites, landing pages, service pages, contact pages, basic SEO setup, domain setup, and Cloudflare Pages deployment.

Can you help with domains or Cloudflare?

Yes. I can help with basic domain setup, DNS questions, Cloudflare Pages, redirects, and connecting a website to a custom domain.

Do you offer one-time help or ongoing support?

Both are possible. Some requests are one-time fixes, while others may need occasional updates, maintenance, troubleshooting, or future improvements.

How do I request a quote?

Use the contact form and describe what you need help with. I’ll review the request and follow up with the best next step.

Do you offer in-person or remote help?

Many things can be handled remotely. Local in-person help may be possible depending on the type of request, location, and schedule.

Do you take online payments?

Not through the website yet. This website is currently focused on information, service requests, and lead/contact requests.

Still have a question?

Send the details and I’ll help you figure out the next step.
