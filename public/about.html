About

I help make technology clearer, cleaner, and easier to use.

Colby’s Tech Life is built around practical problem-solving: websites, data, automation, troubleshooting, and digital support explained in a way regular people can understand.

Hi, I’m Colby.

I work in technology, data, school systems, websites, automation, troubleshooting, and practical digital problem-solving. I enjoy taking something that feels confusing or messy and turning it into something cleaner, simpler, and easier to manage.

My goal is not to make technology feel impressive just for the sake of it. My goal is to make it useful. That might mean building a clean website, organizing a spreadsheet, helping with a domain, setting up a workflow, troubleshooting a problem, or explaining what is going on in plain language.

I care about clear communication, dependable work, and helping people feel less stuck with tech.
