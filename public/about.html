About Colby

I like making technology feel less frustrating and more useful.

Colby's Tech Life is built around practical problem-solving: websites that are easy to understand, reports that are easier to manage, and support that does not make people feel talked down to.

My approach

Clear communication, steady problem-solving, and a practical eye for details.

I work in technology, data, school systems, websites, automation, troubleshooting, and digital problem-solving. A lot of my work comes down to the same thing: understanding the real problem, simplifying the process, and building something people can actually use.

I enjoy helping regular people and small organizations make sense of technology without turning every conversation into a pile of jargon.
