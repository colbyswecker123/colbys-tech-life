About

I help make technology feel manageable.

Colby's Tech Life is built around practical support: clear explanations, useful solutions, and steady help for the technology people actually use.

Colby Swecker

Technology should help you move forward, not slow you down.

I work with technology, data, school systems, websites, automation, troubleshooting, and everyday digital problem-solving. What I enjoy most is taking something that feels confusing or messy and making it clearer, cleaner, and easier to manage.

My approach is simple: listen first, explain clearly, and build practical solutions. Whether it is a small website, a spreadsheet that needs cleanup, a report that needs to make sense, or a tech issue that keeps getting in the way, I focus on what will actually help.
