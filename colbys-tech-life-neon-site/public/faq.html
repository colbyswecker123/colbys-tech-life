FAQ

Common questions before you reach out.

Here are practical answers about the type of help Colby's Tech Life can provide.

What kind of tech help do you offer?

I help with websites, Cloudflare setup, domains, forms, data/reporting, spreadsheets, automation, troubleshooting, email help, device setup, and general digital support.

Do you work with small businesses?

Yes. Colby's Tech Life is a good fit for small businesses, side businesses, churches, nonprofits, local organizations, and individuals who need practical support without a large agency process.

Can you build a website for me?

Yes. I can help create a clean, mobile-friendly informational website that explains what you do, lists your services, and gives people an easy way to contact you.

Can you help with domains or Cloudflare?

Yes. I can help connect domains, explain DNS records, set up Cloudflare Pages, review basic security settings, and prepare a website for future expansion.

Do you offer one-time help or ongoing support?

Both are possible. Some requests are one-time fixes, while others may need recurring updates, maintenance, reporting, or troubleshooting support.

How do I request a quote?

Use the contact page and describe what you need help with. The more detail you include, the easier it is to understand the scope and recommend the next step.

Do you offer in-person or remote help?

Remote help is often the easiest option for websites, data, forms, reporting, and troubleshooting. Local in-person help may be possible depending on the request and schedule.

Is this website an online store?

No. This site is designed for services, information, and lead requests. There is no cart, no payment system, and no customer account system.

Still have a question?

Send a quick message and include what you are trying to solve. I will help point you in the right direction.
